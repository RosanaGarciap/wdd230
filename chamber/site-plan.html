Overview

Site Purpose

  1. Founded on February XX, 20XX, the Guayana's Chamber of Shopping Centers is an independent, non-profit trade association that brings together the main local shopping centers, among which are: Orinokia, Costa del Sol, CC Plaza Atlantico, Macrocentro I, and Macro Centro II, to Promote among associates the subjection of their activities to ethical norms and good practices oriented to the common good of commerces and Guayana's community.

  2. Guayana's Chamber of Shopping Centers has also the goal of provide legal orientation to owners of little bussiness or microservices who desire to set their locals in the Malls. Members of the chamber will have right to participate in decitions that could impact in the continity of their services

Audience

The main target are families, therefore the goal is to provide a healthy enviroment for families to enjoy and spend time toguether all colors and locals inside centers must provide security for all posible family scenarios, for example, parents with children, teenagers, couples, etc.

Typography

Heading Font: Roboto Slap

Slap Serif fonts present a good contrast when paired with serif fonts. Roboto Slab font is a good option that provide style, stetic and well spaced distribution which made it a good option for easy and readable headings.

Paragraph Font: Inter

Inter font was designed to be a secondary typeface for use in paragraph and small UI elements. The design is simple, easy to read and understand, as well as beautiful. Since this website is designed to satify the necesity of both family users and business owners the combination of Roboto Slap and Inter provide a style suitable for both professional and familiar users.
